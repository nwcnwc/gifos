### Q1 — Healing designation chains (sub-classes A1/A2). Design APPROVED. Depends on 3A (promotion becomes a move).
Split into "who designates" and "how the leaf wires" (the split resolves Nathan's
objection that a healer must know the dead seat's links):
- **Who: the witness chain.** If a hole's designated healer is itself dead,
  designation DEVOLVES along a deterministic order **restricted to the hole's
  first-hand neighbour set** (down-child → right-neighbour → remaining row-mates
  cyclically → column-mates). Every candidate held a direct link to the hole, so
  each is an independent first-hand witness (E2/S5 satisfied) with links to
  sponsor from. Confirmed-empty + lower-id-wins absorbs transient double-claims.
- **Nathan's DEPTH RULE (must be law text):** a level-k designee may act only if
  it can **first-hand confirm the hole AND the death of every designee above it**
  — level 2 sees two things directly, level 3 sees three. Devolution on hearsay
  about any link in the chain is forbidden. **The chain never crosses the clique
  boundary**; beyond it the duty falls to resurrection/arrival, never to gossip.
- **SCOPING (agreed):** rows are full cliques *everywhere*, so within-row
  devolution is valid to depth C−1 in **every** section. The COLUMN clique
  (second full witness set, degree 9) and the whole-dead-row resurrection
  handoff are **Section-1 only** (deep sections have no column mesh). Deep
  out-of-row witnesses (down-child, single transpose cross-partner, parent-of-a-
  head) qualify only as first-line witnesses for what they *directly* see; the
  deep whole-row-dead handoff is drain/re-entry (existing behavior). Justified
  by priority: S1 holds every small meeting entirely + every big meeting's door/
  stage/stadium-finish, so it earns the rich machinery.
- **How: self-wire with the healer's free hint.** The healer's job shrinks to
  witness/designate/confirm-empty. *Which* coords the promoted leaf links to is
  geometry (leaf computes it). *Who occupies them* rides as a hint: the healer's
  fill message includes its fresh `coord→occupant` snapshot (bytes it already
  has, zero extra round-trips). *Reaching them* reuses the join/sponsor path
  (S4-signed fill). Under law T the promoted leaf keeps its old links mid-move
  (warm start). Net: link hand-over stops being a concept; **seating, moving,
  and healing become the same primitive** (atomic signed claim into a confirmed-
  empty coord + self-wire), differing only in who designates.
- **A2** = the same devolution applied to H7 resurrection (dead re-seeder →
  next live row downward, wrapped). **State the law ONCE**; admission, healing,
  resurrection all cite it (3A's "devolved admitter" is already this family).
- **Standing guard:** if the sim finds a case where a chain designee can't
  first-hand-confirm, STOP and bring it back for discussion — don't ship it.

## 5. The bug-class taxonomy (the map Q1–Q5 are closing)

The headless-row gap is one instance of a class. Five sub-classes:
- **A. Dangling designation** — the one assigned actor is gone, no named backup
  (healer chains, resurrection recursion, greeter extinction, founder-dies-mid-
  founding). Worst human cost: newcomers locked out; worst variant (founder)
  possibly PERMANENT lockout. Dials barely help — needs Q1/Q4 rule changes.
- **B. Non-atomic moves** — designee exists but is mid-flight (the headless-row
  race; the became-row-mate-cameraless media bug). **Being eliminated by law T
  (3A).** Residue = seconds of gossip staleness (class C) + media re-wiring.
- **C. Clock disagreement** — laws judge "alive" by different freshness rules →
  ghosts, contradictions. Self-heals ≤~2min. Dials help ~2× with a trap (too
  short → a locked phone gets declared dead). Real fix Q3.
- **D. Cadence cliffs** — a transient failure repaired only at the next
  scheduled retry (the `by:null` bug was this). Self-heals 10–40s. Dials ~2×;
  the 10–20× win is event-reactive triggers (Q3).
- **E. Small-N degeneracy** — one device wears every hat; its departure vacates
  every duty at once. The MOST human rooms (1:1, 3-person) are the LEAST
  exercised by churn sweeps. No redundancy to tune into existence — needs
  A/B/D fixes + the Q5 small-N audit.

Dials buy ~one halving concentrated in B/C/D; the two worst outcomes (permanent
lockout in A, broken tiny meetings in E) are exactly what dials CAN'T touch.
